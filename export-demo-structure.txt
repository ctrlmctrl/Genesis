🎯 KEY FEATURES OF THE EXPORTS
==============================

✅ COMPLETE CONTACT INFORMATION
- Full names of all participants
- Phone numbers for easy contact
- Email addresses for communication
- College names for identification

✅ TEAM ORGANIZATION (Team Events)
- Team names clearly displayed
- Team lead identification
- Member roles (Team Lead vs Member)
- Complete team member contact details
- Team payment model: One payment per team (by team lead)
- Team payment status and amount clearly shown

✅ PAYMENT TRACKING
- Payment status (paid, pending, offline_paid)
- Payment method (online, offline)
- Payment identifiers for verification
- Registration type (regular, team, on_spot)

✅ VERIFICATION STATUS
- Check-in status (Verified/Pending)
- Verification timestamps
- Registration dates and times

✅ PROFESSIONAL FORMATTING
- Clean, organized tables
- Proper headers and styling
- Multiple sheets for different data types
- Export timestamps for record keeping

✅ MULTIPLE FORMATS
- Excel files (.xlsx) for data manipulation
- PDF files (.pdf) for sharing and printing
- Both formats include all participant details

This export system provides everything you need for event management, participant communication, and record keeping!
